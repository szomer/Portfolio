About Me

I was born and raised in Amsterdam, the Netherlands, lived in Sweden during my studies, and now located in Thuringia, Germany. (I like to travel around 😉)

Since I was a child I spend my time figuring out my creative side, I always had a keen eye for detail and enjoyed creating art. In secondary school, I started developing an interest in technology when I created my first very own website. I believe art and science go hand in hand with the development of new things.

Now, after completing my bachelor of computer science, I continue to learn web technologies in order to improve my skills, and of course... for fun! While learning, I am actively searching for a job in the web development field.
