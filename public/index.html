About Me

I am born and raised in Amsterdam, the Netherlands, and lived in Sweden during my studies. Since I was a child I spend my time figuring out my creative side, I always had an eye for detail and enjoyed creating art. Later on, in secondary school, I started developing an interest for technology when I had to create my first very own website. I believe art and science go hand in hand with the development of new things.
After completing my bachelor of computer science, I continue to learn web technologies to improve my skills, and of course.. for fun! While learning I am actively searching for a job in the web development field.
